About

Independent analysis for prediction markets.

PredictionGate is a media and analysis company built to help readers understand what forecasting markets are pricing and why those probabilities move.

What it is

PredictionGate covers prediction market news, event contracts, forecasting platforms, and market probabilities with a focus on clarity and context.

Why it exists

Markets can move faster than traditional coverage. PredictionGate explains those moves so readers can understand the signal, the uncertainty, and the next catalyst.

What it covers

Politics, economics, technology, sports, world events, culture, regulated markets, forecasting communities, and the infrastructure around them.

What it does not do

PredictionGate is not a prediction market, brokerage, sportsbook, financial adviser, legal adviser, or investment service.

Editorial principles

Accuracy

Report probabilities, dates, and platform context carefully.

Transparency

Label demonstration data, uncertainty, and sourcing limits clearly.

Clear sourcing

Point readers toward the evidence behind a move whenever possible.

Corrections

Correct material errors promptly and visibly.

Fact and opinion

Separate reported facts from interpretation and analysis.
